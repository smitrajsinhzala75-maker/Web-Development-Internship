Who is he?

The Greatest of All Time

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, into a working-class family. His father, José Dinis Aveiro, worked as a municipal gardener, and his mother, Maria Dolores, was a cook.

From a young age, Ronaldo showed extraordinary talent and dedication. He left his home island at age 12 to join Sporting CP's youth academy in Lisbon, living away from his family — a sacrifice that would shape his relentless mentality.

Ronaldo is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers in history. His pace, athleticism, technical skill, aerial ability, and clinical finishing make him a complete forward. But it is his obsessive work ethic and never-say-die attitude that truly set him apart.

Off the pitch, he is a devoted father to five children and one of the most recognised faces on the planet — with over 600 million Instagram followers, the most of any person on earth.
